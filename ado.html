Eucharistic Adoration
In Aeternum
·
Bring with you to the altar
Intercession
Hold your loved ones before the Blessed Sacrament in silent prayer.
Reparation
Offer your hour in reparation for sins — your own, and the world's.
Surrender
Lay down what you cannot carry. Let Him hold it for you.
Lectio
Open your heart to receive a word from the Lord as you adore.
Thanksgiving
Count your blessings before the Lamb who gave everything.
Consecration
Offer yourself wholly — through Mary, to Jesus in the Eucharist.
